Welcome to Connect Four Trio

Connect Four Click is the classic version of Connect Four. It can be played on PC using mouse or on phone.

Connect Four Voice is the version of Connect Four using voice to decide where to put a piece.

Connect Four Face is the version of Connect Four using facial recognition to decide where to put a piece.

To play Connect Four Voice or Face, please take a minute to read the instructions page first.
